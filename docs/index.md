## mTB targets repository

Storing information about different target enzymes found in mycobacterium tuberculosis with and emphasis on applications in computation chemistry.

For each target, we are trying to provide:

- information about the target and role in mTB
- available x-ray structures (apo and co-crystals)
- natural substrates and co-factors
- commerical drugs
- other known inhibitors
- SMILES strings for small molecules
- Fully setup structures for molecular dynamics or docking
